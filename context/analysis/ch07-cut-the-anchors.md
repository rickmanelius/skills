# Chapter 7: Cut The Anchors

## Chapter Summary
Removing what drags you down: fear, toxic people/habits, resource scarcity mindset. Four structured exercises that move from diagnosis to action.

## Candidate Skills

### `/fear-setting`
- **What it does**: Tim Ferriss-style fear setting — write down worst-case scenarios, scope consequences, brainstorm mitigations, rate each on effort/pain/likelihood (0-10).
- **Universality**: 9 | **Power**: 9 | **AI-transferability**: 9 | **Total**: 27
- **Key insight**: "Things that felt like a 7 to a 9 end up being a 2 to a 5."
- **Key quote**: "Hard choices, easy life. Easy choices, hard life." — Jerzy Gregorek

### `/toxicity-audit`
- **What it does**: Systematic identification and removal of toxic people, places, habits, and things dragging down performance.
- **Universality**: 8 | **Power**: 9 | **AI-transferability**: 7 | **Total**: 24
- **Key story**: Firing one toxic person led to an immediate standing ovation and sustained productivity boost.
- **Key framework**: Google's Project Aristotle — psychological safety and equal participation are the two key factors.

### `/resource-audit`
- **What it does**: Surfaces hidden resources across five dimensions: People, Ideas/Answers, Skills, Experiences, and Freestyle.
- **Universality**: 9 | **Power**: 8 | **AI-transferability**: 9 | **Total**: 26
- **Key reframe**: Tony Robbins — the common denominator for not achieving goals is always "Resources" — but the real answer is resourcefulness.
- **Key quote** (Harvey Specter): "You take the gun or pull out a bigger gun or you call a bluff or you do any of the other 146 other things."

### `/commitment-replacement`
- **What it does**: After removing something toxic, intentionally design replacement commitments, norms, and guardrails to prevent backsliding.
- **Universality**: 7 | **Power**: 8 | **AI-transferability**: 8 | **Total**: 23
- **Key framework**: 6-step rollout + 5 intervention points (job posting, interview, HR reviews, retros, leadership empowerment)
