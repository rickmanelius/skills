# Chapter 10: Hit the Gas

## Chapter Summary
The operational playbook chapter — 8 SOPs covering execution, visibility, bottlenecks, delegation, scoping, and adoption. The densest chapter with the most discrete skills.

## Candidate Skills

### `/traffic-jam-audit`
- **What it does**: Diagnoses organizational gridlock by categorizing work into green (revenue), blue (internal with ROI), red (internal no ROI), then triaging ruthlessly.
- **Universality**: 8 | **Power**: 9 | **AI-transferability**: 8 | **Total**: 25
- **Key result**: Freed 2-10 hours/week per person across 50 people = $1.25M/year recovered.

### `/paint-done`
- **What it does**: Structured collaborative exercise where requester describes "done" in detail and implementer asks clarifying questions until both share a clear vision.
- **Universality**: 10 | **Power**: 9 | **AI-transferability**: 10 | **Total**: 29
- **Key quote**: "Five minutes spent here can save hours or days in Figma or a code editor."
- **Key insight**: Same spec given to 10 designers yields 10 wildly different results without painting done.
