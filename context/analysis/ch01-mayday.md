# Chapter 1: Mayday — "We Are Shutting You Down"

## Chapter Summary
Primarily narrative and scene-setting. Tells the story of the author's web agency being shut down for back taxes, introduces 9 "Startup Hazards," and establishes credentials through turnaround war stories. Light on structured frameworks, heavy on motivational context.
