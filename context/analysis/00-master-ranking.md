# Master Ranking: Top Skills from Save Your Startup

## Methodology

50 candidate skills were identified across 12 chapters. Each was scored on three dimensions (1-10):
- **Universality**: How applicable beyond the book's specific context? Would founders across industries use this?
- **Power**: How impactful when applied correctly?
- **AI-transferability**: How well can an AI guide someone through this? (Structured frameworks score high, purely experiential advice scores low)

Skills were then evaluated for additional factors:
- **Distinctiveness**: Does this skill offer something unique, or is it a generic framework available everywhere?
- **Book DNA**: Does this skill carry the book's distinctive voice and philosophy (kind over nice, mentor-guided walkthroughs)?
- **Composability**: Can standalone skills be combined into natural sequences without losing their individual value?

## Recommended Top 12: The Save Your Startup Skill Set

After scoring, I applied three additional filters to break ties and select the final 12:

1. **Does it carry the book's DNA?** Skills that feel like a Techstars mentor walking you through a hard conversation scored higher than generic frameworks.
2. **Is it distinct from what's freely available?** RACI and Tuckman's model are well-known; the book's unique spin on them matters, but pure originals ranked higher.
3. **Does it cover a unique phase of the journey?** The final 12 should span the full arc: diagnosis → product → numbers → story → team → mindset → execution → mastery.

### Why These 12

**Full journey coverage**: The 12 skills span the book's complete arc:
- **Am I the right founder?** → `/founder-type-check`, `/capability-willingness-matrix`
- **Is this the right product?** → `/wiifm-check`
- **Do the numbers work?** → `/napkin-math`, `/assumptions-audit`
- **Can I tell the story?** → `/five-finger-pitch`
- **What's holding me back?** → `/fear-setting`, `/ruthless-elimination`
- **How do I execute?** → `/paint-done`, `/break-bottleneck`, `/high-medium-low`
- **How do I plan?** → `/chunk`

**Book DNA is strong**: Every skill embodies the "kind over nice" mentor approach — direct questions that force honest answers. `/founder-type-check` asks "are you delusional?" `/fear-setting` says "write down the worst that can happen." `/napkin-math` says "let's do the math and see if your dream survives arithmetic."

**AI-transferability is excellent**: 10 of the 12 score 9+ on AI-transferability. These are structured walkthroughs with clear questions, checklists, and scoring criteria — exactly what AI excels at delivering.
