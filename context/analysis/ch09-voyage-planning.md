# Chapter 9: Voyage Planning — Connecting Next Actions to North Star

## Chapter Summary
Goal decomposition from vision to daily action. The bridge between strategic thinking and tactical execution. Three tightly connected frameworks that form a natural sequence.

## Candidate Skills

### `/north-star`
- **What it does**: Define long-term vision (aspirational world state) and mission (specific manner of achieving it) to form an audacious north star.
- **Universality**: 9 | **Power**: 8 | **AI-transferability**: 7 | **Total**: 24
- **Key story**: The carrier cook — "I cook the food so that our pilots get the nutrition they need... so we can win the war."

### `/chunk`
- **What it does**: Systematically breaks a large goal down across time horizons (decade → year → quarter → month → week → day), creating a connected hierarchy.
- **Universality**: 10 | **Power**: 9 | **AI-transferability**: 9 | **Total**: 28
- **Key framework**: Time horizon hierarchy — Decades: vision, Decade: mission, Annual: goals, Quarterly: strategy/milestones, Monthly: tactics/KPIs, Weekly: targets, Daily: efforts
- **Key question**: "In order to achieve X by Y, what has to be true in one day, one week, one month, one quarter, and one year?"

### `/next-action`
- **What it does**: Determines the single most valuable thing to work on right now by connecting daily actions upward through weekly targets to monthly milestones.
- **Universality**: 9 | **Power**: 8 | **AI-transferability**: 9 | **Total**: 26
- **Three filtering questions**: What needs to happen this week for the monthly target? Today for the weekly target? Next for today's goals?

### `/pivot-check`
- **What it does**: Evaluates whether strategy needs adjustment by checking hypotheses against market feedback. North star stays stable while tactics flex.
- **Universality**: 7 | **Power**: 8 | **AI-transferability**: 7 | **Total**: 22
- **Key quote**: "Fall in love with the problem, not the solution."
