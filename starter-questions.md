# Starter Questions for Rapid Testing

2-3 sample prompts per skill to quickly test each one.

---

## /paint-done

1. `/paint-done I want to launch an MVP of my SaaS product by end of Q2`
2. `/paint-done We need to hire our first sales rep`
3. `/paint-done Redesigning our onboarding flow to reduce churn`

## /assumptions-audit

1. `/assumptions-audit We're projecting $1M ARR by month 18 with a freemium model`
2. `/assumptions-audit revenue funnel — we assume 3% of free trial users convert to paid`
3. `/assumptions-audit COGS — we're estimating $0.50/user/month for infrastructure`

## /chunk

1. `/chunk Build a $10M ARR developer tools company in 5 years`
2. `/chunk Go from solo founder to a team of 10 by end of year`
3. `/chunk Launch and grow a community-led growth engine for our open source project`

## /capability-willingness-matrix

1. `/capability-willingness-matrix CTO role at my startup — I'm a first-time technical founder`
2. `/capability-willingness-matrix I need to decide whether I should be doing sales myself or hiring`
3. `/capability-willingness-matrix Evaluating whether I'm the right person to lead fundraising`

## /fear-setting

1. `/fear-setting I need to let go of my co-founder but I'm afraid it'll kill the company`
2. `/fear-setting Pivoting away from our current product that has paying customers`
3. `/fear-setting Quitting my job to go full-time on my startup`

## /ruthless-elimination

1. `/ruthless-elimination my calendar this week — I'm working 70 hours and nothing important is getting done`
2. `/ruthless-elimination I'm the bottleneck on every decision and can't find time for strategy`
3. `/ruthless-elimination Our team of 5 is spread across 12 different initiatives`

## /napkin-math

1. `/napkin-math We need to hit $100K MRR in 12 months with a $49/mo product`
2. `/napkin-math Can we build a viable business selling $9/mo subscriptions to indie developers?`
3. `/napkin-math Our marketplace needs 1000 active sellers — how many do we need to recruit per week?`

## /founder-type-check

1. `/founder-type-check I've been working on this idea for 6 months but haven't talked to customers yet`
2. `/founder-type-check I just got rejected from YC and I'm questioning everything`
3. `/founder-type-check We raised a seed round but I'm not sure I'm the right CEO for the next stage`

## /wiifm-check

1. `/wiifm-check Our AI-powered meeting summarizer for remote teams`
2. `/wiifm-check A project management tool designed specifically for agencies`
3. `/wiifm-check We built a browser extension that auto-categorizes bookmarks`

## /five-finger-pitch

1. `/five-finger-pitch We're building Stripe for climate credits`
2. `/five-finger-pitch My company helps restaurants reduce food waste with AI demand forecasting`
3. `/five-finger-pitch An open-source alternative to Notion for developer documentation`

## /break-bottleneck

1. `/break-bottleneck All customer onboarding goes through me and I can't keep up`
2. `/break-bottleneck Our deploy process takes 3 days because of manual QA`
3. `/break-bottleneck We're generating leads but deals stall at the proposal stage`

## /high-medium-low

1. `/high-medium-low Adding a team collaboration feature to our solo-user product`
2. `/high-medium-low Building an API for third-party integrations`
3. `/high-medium-low Migrating from a monolith to microservices`
